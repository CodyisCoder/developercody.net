### Version 0.5.7 - Enhanced User Experience and UI Improvements

**New Features & Enhancements:**

- **Search Functionality Added to the Games Tab:**  
  To improve usability and efficiency, a search bar has been integrated into the Games tab. Users can now quickly locate their desired games by typing keywords, making navigation faster and more intuitive.

- **Revamped Secret/IP Section:**  
  The Secret/IP section has undergone a complete visual overhaul, providing a more polished and modern look. With this update, the design is sleeker, more user-friendly, and aesthetically aligned with the overall UI, giving users a cooler and more dynamic experience when accessing sensitive information.
